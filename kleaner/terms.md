## Description of Service

Kleaner is a photo management app that helps you review and delete unwanted photos stored on your device. The app:

- Allows you to mark photos for deletion

## Photo Deletion

**Important:** When you delete photos through Kleaner, they are permanently removed from your photo library.

Deleted photos may still be recoverable from the “Recently Deleted” album in the Photos app for a limited time, depending on your device settings.

**You are solely responsible for the photos you choose to delete.**  
We strongly recommend reviewing all photos carefully before confirming deletion.
