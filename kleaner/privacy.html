# Privacy Policy **Kleaner — Photo Cleaner** *Last updated: January 2026* ## Overview Kleaner is designed with privacy as a core principle. Your photos never leave your device, and we collect minimal data to provide the service. ## What We Don't Collect - **Your photos** — All photo processing happens locally on your device. We never upload, view, analyze, or store your photos on any server. - **Photo metadata** — We don't collect EXIF data, location information, or any details about your photos. - **Personal information** — We don't require an account, email, or any personal details to use Kleaner. ## What We Collect ### Analytics (Anonymous) We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage data to improve the app: - App crashes and errors - General usage patterns (e.g., which features are used most) - Device type and iOS version This data cannot be used to identify you personally. ### Subscription Data If you subscribe to Kleaner Pro: - Purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store - We receive confirmation of your subscription status from Apple - We do not have access to your payment information ## Data Storage All app data (preferences, progress, statistics) is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, this data is removed. ## Third-Party Services - **Apple App Store** — Handles all purchases and subscriptions - **Apple StoreKit** — Manages subscription verification ## Children's Privacy Kleaner does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. ## Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date. ## Contact If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: **Email:** [apps@doohyper.com] --- © 2026 doohyper.
