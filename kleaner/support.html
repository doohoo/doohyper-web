Frequently Asked Questions

Are my photos uploaded anywhere?

No. All photo processing happens locally on your device. Your photos never leave your phone.

Can I recover deleted photos?

Photos deleted through Kleaner are moved to the "Recently Deleted" album in your Photos app. They remain there for up to 30 days before being permanently removed.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → select Kleaner → Cancel Subscription.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open Kleaner, go to Settings within the app, and tap "Restore Purchases".
